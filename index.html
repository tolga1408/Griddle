How to play
Solve the waffle by swapping letters. There are six hidden 5-letter words: three across and three down.
  • Green: correct letter, correct position.
  • Yellow: this letter belongs in one of that tile’s words, but somewhere else.
  • Grey: this letter does not help this tile’s current words.
You get 15 swaps. A new date-seeded puzzle appears every day.
